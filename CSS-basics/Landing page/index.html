The hidden gem of Central Europe

Brno, Czech Republic

Brno is the Czech Republic's second largest city known for its great beer, beautiful architecture, and international atmosphere.

Find out more

Check out some of the mosts popular places in Brno

Brno is full of valuable historical buildings and interesting places. For a true feel of just how amazing the city is, you should definitely visit the two dominant hills in the city center. On Petrov Hill, you can admire the Cathedral of St Peter and Paul, and at the top of Špilberk hill, you'll find the city's famous castle, which contains a museum and casemates. It was known as the harshest jail in the Austro-Hungarian Empire.
